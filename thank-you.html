What Happens Next

Three Things Before We Talk

Nothing complicated — just a quick rundown of what to expect on your call and how to make the most of it.

01

Calendar Invite

You'll get a calendar invite in your inbox within the next few minutes. It includes a phone number (or video link) and a quick reminder before the call.

02

The Call

About 15 minutes. Your advisor will walk through plans, fleet access, the Tampa club, and answer anything else that comes up. No pressure either way.

03

If It's a Fit

If membership makes sense for you, we can handle everything on the same call — including your first reservation. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you honestly.

While You Wait

A Few Things Worth Skimming

Most of our members come into the call already knowing the basics. Here's what's helpful to look at beforehand so we can spend most of your 15 minutes on your specific questions:

  • The five plans — most people land between the 50-hour and 12-month plans depending on how often they boat.
  • The Tampa fleet — flats boats for the shallows, pontoons for family days, dual consoles, center consoles, and twin-engine consoles for offshore.
  • Reciprocal access — your membership works at every Gulf Stream location: Tampa, St. Pete – Tierra Verde, Bradenton, and our East Coast clubs.
  • How reservations work — book two to eight hours at a time, hold up to four reservations on the calendar.
